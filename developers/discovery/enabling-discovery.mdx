---

Enabling **Discovery** for your app makes it available in the [App Directory](/developers/discovery/overview#app-directory) and [App Launcher](/developers/discovery/overview#app-launcher) for users to search for and install.

---

## Step 1: Verify your app

To enable Discovery, the team owner must complete identity and application verification.

**App verification** also unlocks [monetization features](/developers/monetization/overview) such as in-app purchases and subscriptions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Visit the [Developer Portal](https://discord.com/developers/applications) and select your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open App Verification">
    Select **App Verification** from the left-hand menu to see the verification requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the verification criteria">
    Complete all listed qualification criteria for your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for verification">
    Once requirements are met, submit your app for verification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For more details, read the [How Do I Get My App Verified?](https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/23926564536471-How-Do-I-Get-My-App-Verified) Help Center article.

---

## Step 2: Opt into discovery

After verifying your app, opt into Discovery in the Developer Portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Visit the [Developer Portal](https://discord.com/developers/applications) and select your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Discovery Status">
    Select **Discovery → Discovery Status** from the left-hand menu to see the requirements for enabling discovery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the discovery criteria">
    Complete all listed Discovery qualification criteria for your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add app metadata and images">
    Under **Discovery → Discovery Settings**, customize your app's name, description, images, and other details that appear in discovery surfaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable discovery">
    Once all requirements are met, enable **Discovery** for your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
After enabling Discovery, it may take up to **24 hours** for your app to appear in the App Directory and App Launcher.
</Info>

For more information, read the [How Can Users Discover and Play My Activity?](https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/21204493235991-How-Can-Users-Discover-and-Play-My-Activity) Help Center article.

### Check that your app is discoverable

1. Go to the [App Directory](https://discord.com/application-directory) and search for your app by name.
2. If it appears, your app is live in discovery.
3. If it doesn't appear, confirm you've completed all steps above and wait up to 24 hours.
