## Key provisions summary

The following is a summary of the Developer ToS. Always refer to the full text for authoritative legal terms.

### License to use the API

Discord grants you a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to access and use the Discord API solely to develop apps that interact with the Discord platform in accordance with the terms.

### Acceptable use

- Use the API only as documented and permitted.
- Do not reverse engineer, modify, or create derivative works of the Discord client or undocumented APIs.
- Do not exceed rate limits or circumvent usage controls.
- Do not use the API in ways that damage, disable, or impair the Discord service.

### Your responsibilities

- You are responsible for all activity that occurs through your app's credentials.
- You must keep your credentials (bot token, client secret) secure and confidential.
- You must comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
- You are responsible for the content and behavior of your app and its users.

### Data handling

- Your Privacy Policy must accurately describe your data collection and usage practices to users.
- You must handle user data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
- Discord may audit your data handling practices for compliance.

### Termination

Discord may suspend or terminate your API access at any time if you violate the Developer Terms of Service or Developer Policy. Discord may also modify or discontinue API features with reasonable notice.

### Changes to the terms

Discord may update the Developer Terms of Service. Continued use of the API after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
